# Competitive Research Notes

Date: 2026-04-22

## Benchmarks Reviewed

- Xero UK: simple SME language, visible business outcomes, pricing/resource pathways, strong trust and award signals.
- Tide UK: direct small-business CTA language and clear product navigation.
- Enterprise Nation: practical UK SME tone, especially around AI adoption and productivity.
- Kalungi: B2B SaaS fractional CMO positioning, clear "what you get" structure, agency comparison, and case-story proof.
- Fractional CMO/SaaS competitor pages: proof-led case study grids and repeated low-friction booking CTAs.

## Borrowed Patterns

- Use plain-English lead magnets that help a founder self-diagnose before booking.
- Put qualification up front with a "not a fit" route instead of treating every visitor as a sales lead.
- Make proof readable as before / work done / result.
- Keep CTAs consistent and repeated without adding new variants.
- Make writing and resources discoverable by topic for Google and AI answer engines.
